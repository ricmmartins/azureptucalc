================================================================================
PRICING PIPELINE (4-TIER PRIORITY)
================================================================================
1. Custom Override — user-entered rates in custom pricing panel
2. Live Azure API — real-time from Azure Retail Prices API (3-hour cache)
3. Official Hardcoded — officialPTUPricing.js + official_token_pricing.js
4. Fallback — enhanced_pricing_service.js fallback + external_pricing_config.json

getCurrentPricing() in App.jsx (~line 619) implements this cascade.

AZURE PTU PRICING (verified March 2025)
================================================================================
Deployment   | Hourly(On-Demand) | Monthly Res. | 1-Year Res.(annual) | 1-Year(monthly)
-------------|-------------------|--------------|---------------------|----------------
Global       | $1.00             | $260         | $2,652              | $221
Data Zone    | $1.10             | $286         | $2,916              | $243
Regional     | $2.00             | $286         | $2,916              | $243

CRITICAL: Azure offers Monthly and 1-Year PTU reservations ONLY. NO 3-year reservation.

Discount percentages (vs on-demand hourly):
- Monthly Reservation: ~64% off
- 1-Year Reservation: ~70% off
- 1-Year vs Monthly: ~15% additional savings
